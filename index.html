⚖️
Tiebreaker: If two or more pool teams finish with identical points at the end of the tournament, the tiebreaker is the total number of goals scored by all of that team's countries combined throughout the tournament. Most goals wins.
🐍
Snake Draft Format: 12 players, 48 countries, 4 picks each. Draft order reverses each round (1→12, then 12→1, then 1→12, then 12→1). The person picking 12th and 13th overall (picks back-to-back in the snake) gets a natural fairness advantage built into the format — combined with the in-game scoring system where any country can rack up points, late-round picks can absolutely win the pool.
